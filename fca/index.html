The Problem

Marketplace leads are time-sensitive. Most agencies aren't built for that.

When a family submits an inquiry on A Place for Mom, Caring.com, or a similar platform, they hear from multiple agencies at the same time. The agency that reaches them first builds trust and sets expectations before anyone else gets a chance.

But most home care agencies don't have a dedicated person watching for incoming leads around the clock. By the time the email is seen and a call is made, another agency has already had a full conversation with the family.

⏱️

Delayed response

Leads sit in an inbox until someone checks it. By then, the window to make a first impression has closed.

📵

After-hours gaps

Families often submit inquiries in the evening or on weekends. Most agencies aren't staffed to respond until Monday morning.

💸

Wasted lead spend

Agencies pay hundreds of dollars per referral lead, then lose the conversion because response time lags behind the competition.

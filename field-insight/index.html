Two Ways to Contribute

A Trail Guide Review or a Field Note

A Trail Guide Review is a structured rating across six categories, the same format families see on the platform. Partial reviews are welcome. You don't need to rate every category if you don't have a strong view on all of them.

A Field Note is completely free-form. Something you heard, noticed, or observed that families probably wouldn't learn on a tour. Field Notes are never displayed publicly; they give our AI more context when helping families compare communities.

You can submit both in one form.

Your Contribution Matters

Anonymous. Impactful. Appreciated.

Your name is never attached to any submission on the Compass Place platform. You can share honest observations, even critical ones, without concern for professional relationships.

For every ten submissions we receive from our expert network, Compass Place makes a donation to a local senior care-focused nonprofit. Your insight contributes to something larger than any single family's decision.

If you have a specific community you've visited recently, a brief observation goes a long way.

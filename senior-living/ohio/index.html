Why Ohio uses area pages instead of city pages

In this market, local comparison sets make more sense than strict municipal boundaries. Families often compare Dublin with nearby Plain City options, or Hilliard with west-side Columbus communities, so area pages are a better fit for how people actually browse.

If a future Ohio area becomes too large or too fragmented, city pages can always be added later. They are not necessary just for the sake of symmetry with Indiana.
